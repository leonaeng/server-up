How to get a girlfriend in GTA 5?

GTA 5 gives your character the chance to make a girlfriend in the game. Follow these steps to get one:

  • Head over to a strip club and ask for a stripper to a private dance
  • Flirt with the stripper to increase your Like meter
  • Avoid the bouncer catching you flirt, will be thrown out
  • When the Like meter is full, the stripper will ask you to meet outside
  • Select Go Home and wait for her outside the strip club
  • Get in the car and follow the given route
  • Hang out at her place, to add her contact number to your phone
  • Use the number to call and see her again

Is there any unlimited ammo cheat for GTA 5?

No, GTA 5 does not have any cheat to get unlimited ammo. You can use the weapon cheat, TOOLUP, to get new weapons will full ammo.
