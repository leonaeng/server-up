How to use a Photo Editor?

  1. Open the editor and a photo

    To get started, a photo from a computer or from Facebook, after that you can use a large number of editing tools. Also remember that our photo editor does not store your photos, so you can be calm about your privacy.

  2. Use photo editing tools

    All the tools of our photo editor are divided into 6 main categories - basic tools, filters, overlays, frames, text and clipart. Determine which tool you need and find it in the menu list, then follow the tool settings.

  3. Save the photo to a computer or share it

    After you finish editing, you can share the photo to your social. You can also use the settings to select the image quality and file format (png or jpg).
