Artificial intelligence and its capabilities in Photoshop

Adobe were among the first to use artificial intelligence for photo processing. Such intelligence is the basis of several tools of the editor Photoshop, for example: different filters, content-aware fill, selection of objects. In this section, let's look at a few of these functions - you can try them on your own images to understand how they work in the editor.

To make it easier to master working in Photoshop

  • You should not try to understand all the functions at once. It is better to start with a specific task and study tools only for it, for example:
  • if you need to retouch photos - master "Stamp", "Band-Aid", selection, color correction blocks;
  • to draw something - brushes, Eraser, Stamp;
  • Make layouts for the stories - shapes, layers, fonts.
  • So, moving in small steps from task to task, in a week or two you can feel confident in one of the blocks and start mastering the next one.
